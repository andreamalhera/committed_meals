# Ceviche Ã  la Christa
Guatemalan ceviche

* 400g vegan shrimps
* 3 diced tomatoes
* 1 chopped onion
* coriander
* lime juice
* 1 diced avocado
* worcester sauce
* salt and pepper
* soda crackers for the side

Mix all ingredients up to chopped onions. Condiment with lime juice, coriander, worcester sauce, salt and pepper. 
Serve with crackers on the side and enjoy!
