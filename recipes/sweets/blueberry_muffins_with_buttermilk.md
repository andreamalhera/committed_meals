# Blueberry Muffins with Buttermilk

* 60 g butter
* 85 g sugar
* 1/2 tsp. vanilla sugar
* 1 egg
* 60 ml buttermilk
* 1/4 tsp. rubbed lemon peel
* 110 g flour
* 10 g corn starch
* 1/2 tsp. backing powder
* 1/4 tsp. salt
* 1 pinch of cinammon
* 125 g blueberries
* far or parchment paper for baking sheet

Melt down butter and mix 75 g sugar and vanilla sugar. Add egg and buttermilk. Mix flour, corn starch, baking powder, salt and cinammon and mix it in. Preheat oven to 180 Â°C and prepare baking sheet. Mix blueberries into dough. Fill cupcake forms to 2/3 and sprinkle with rest of powder sugar. Bake them for 25 min, serve and enjoy!
