# Paris Salad

* frensch goat cheese
* walnuts
* colorful peppers (Paprika)
* seedless blue grapes
* sweet honey mustard dressing

Wash and cut lettuce, mix lettuce and rest of the dry ingredients in a bowl. Add dressing, mix, serve and enjoy fresh!
