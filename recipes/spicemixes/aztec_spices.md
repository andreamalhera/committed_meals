# Aztec power powder

* 4 tbsp chili powder
* 1 tbsp salt
* 1 tsp garlic
* 1 tsp onion powder
* 1 tsp paprika (noble sweet)
* 1 tsp marjoram
* 1 tsp oregano (dried)
* 1 tsp coriander powder
* 1 tsp cayenne pepper

Mix all ingredients with a mortar. Use to spice mesoamerican dishes, stews and more. Serve and enjoy!
